Our Story

Growing up, I didn’t have much. Money was tight, and expensive, trendy clothes were out of reach. But that never stopped me from dreaming. I admired fashion influencers, scrolling through their stylish outfits, wishing I could afford the same looks. That’s when the idea hit me—why should great fashion only be for those who can spend a fortune?

With that thought, TrendyStyles was born. I set out on a mission to create a clothing brand that offers the same high-quality, stylish pieces seen on influencers—but at prices that anyone can afford. I researched fabrics, manufacturers, and trends, determined to build something that people like me—people who love fashion but don’t want to break the bank—could be proud to wear.

Today, TrendyStyles isn’t just a brand; it’s a movement. It’s for the trendsetters, the dreamers, and the go-getters who refuse to settle. No one should have to choose between looking good and staying on budget. Thank you for being part of this journey. Let’s make fashion accessible for all!

Our Journey

2023: The Beginning

When the pandemic ended, me and Janessa were brainstorming ideas to start a business, Thats when Janessa gave a bright idea to create TrendyStyles.

2024: Online Launch

The TrendyStyles website is officially launched in July of 2024. Thanks to influencers, we were able to gather over 500+ orders in 2 weeks.

2025: Present

TrendyStyles has now launched the first official branch located in Pasig City.
